Girls Who Code is a national organization focusing on improving engagement with STEM subjects among girls in all grades. The Erwin Middle School GWC chapter begins the year with a coding introduction, then girls pick a topic and project designed to improve the lives of people in our community. As the year progresses, the students build their skills, assign the workflow, and complete their project.
